Sponsor: Lexus RC F on 20-Inch Vossen Forged LC-107 Wheels

Posted by krew on August 23rd, 2016

Here we go — this Ultrasonic Blue Lexus RC F is outfitted with hand-brushed Vossen Forged LC-107 wheels in Space Grey:

Measuring 20×9.5 and 20×11 and wrapped in Toyo Tires, these wheels were specifically engineered for the RC F. Big shoutout to the owner Kevin Josephs and photographer William Stern!
